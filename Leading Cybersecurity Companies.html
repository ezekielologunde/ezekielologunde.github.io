Service Category Breakdown

1. Next-Gen SOC (Detection & Response)

Focuses on visibility across the entire enterprise stack.

  • SIEM/SOAR: Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, IBM QRadar.
  • XDR: Extended detection across email, cloud, and network.

2. Identity & Access Management (IAM)

"Identity is the new perimeter."

  • Leaders: Okta, Microsoft Entra ID, Ping Identity.
  • Services: Multi-Factor Auth (MFA), Single Sign-On (SSO), Privileged Access (PAM).

3. Zero Trust & SASE

Replacing traditional VPNs with secure, identity-based tunnels.

  • Leaders: Zscaler, Netskope, Cloudflare.

Current Industry Trends

  • 🚀 AI-First Security: Companies are shifting from signature-based detection to Generative AI models that predict attacker behavior.
  • ☁️ CNAPP: Cloud Native Application Protection Platforms are becoming the standard for AWS/Azure/GCP users.
  • 📉 Consolidation: Enterprise customers are moving away from "best-of-breed" point products toward unified security platforms (e.g., Palo Alto's "Platformization").
