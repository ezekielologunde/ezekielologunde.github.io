IT & Cybersecurity Basics

The ultimate foundation for high school and college students looking to break into the tech industry.

What is IT?

Think of IT as the nervous system of the modern world. Professionals build and maintain the networks, servers, and devices that allow society to function digitally.

  • • Cloud Infrastructure
  • • Network Administration
  • • Hardware Troubleshooting

What is Cybersecurity?

If IT is the nervous system, Cybersecurity is the immune system. It protects digital health by identifying and neutralizing threats like malware and hackers.

  • • Threat Hunting
  • • Ethical Hacking
  • • Incident Response

Core Foundations

Networking (The Connections)

How devices talk. Key terms: IP Addresses, DNS (the internet's phonebook), and the OSI Model.

Operating Systems

The bridge between user and hardware. Master Windows and learn to love Linux (the industry standard).

Cryptography

The art of secret writing. Encryption ensures your private data stays private using mathematical keys.

Identity Management

Ensuring people are who they say they are using MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication) and Biometrics.
