About

Profile

I am a cybersecurity professional, educator, and researcher with a demonstrated record of building and scaling rigorous, industry-aligned academic programs at community college, university, and K–12 levels.

Currently pursuing a Doctor of Engineering in Cybersecurity Analytics at George Washington University, my research examines AI-enabled security governance frameworks, adversarial machine learning defenses, and risk quantification in enterprise and educational environments.

As Founder of Cyntraix, I provide enterprise security assessments, Zero Trust architecture design, and risk governance advisory services to organizations navigating NIST CSF, CMMC 2.0, and HITRUST compliance landscapes.

I bring deep experience in curriculum governance, faculty collaboration, departmental strategic planning, and workforce development — committed to academic excellence, student equity, and advancing the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity defense.
