üéØ Choosing the Right Path

For Beginners

If you are just starting out, this is a great path to follow:

  1. Start with **Cisco Intro to Cybersecurity** for foundational knowledge.
  2. Move to **TryHackMe** for hands-on, practical skills.
  3. Pursue the **ISC¬≤ CC** certification for an entry-level credential.

For Students

This path is tailored for those currently enrolled in school:

  1. Enroll in **CodePath** if available for structured learning.
  2. Supplement your studies with **TryHackMe** for hands-on practice.
  3. Consider the **Google Cybersecurity Certificate** to boost your resume.

For Career Changers

This path is ideal if you are transitioning into a cybersecurity career:

  1. Begin with the **Google Cybersecurity Certificate** for a solid foundation.
  2. Add **TryHackMe** to build practical skills.
  3. Pursue relevant certifications, such as **CompTIA Security+**, for career advancement.
